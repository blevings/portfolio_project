About Us

What We Do

Meat Your Way is a Farm to Table supplier of beef for your family cut to your specifications. Our Angus based crossbred cattle have been selected to provide calves that produce meat in a natural and sustainable way. Our processor is hand chosen to provide the quality and customer service we demand.

Our Offering

We offer grass fed, grain finished beef that is custom processed for you. It is aged properly and vacuum sealed for long life storage in your freezer. We sell by carcass weight and you can buy quarter, half, or whole beef carcasses. The meat is cut to your specifications for your family's enjoyment. This will allow you to trim your protein costs and ensure you know the source.

Why Choose Us?

We have raised and consumed our own beef for 36 years and have decided to offer you the opportunity to feed your family in the same way. We have learned the art of feeding high quality cattle without the use of hormones or stimulants. We will provide you the same quality our family consumes every day. Once you eat this beef, there's no turning back. It's simply that good!
